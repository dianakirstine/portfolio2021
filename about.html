About me

My name is Diana Kirstine Koroma. I’m 24 years old and living in Copenhagen. I started studying multimedia design at Zealand Erhvervsakademi in summer of 2021, and have been pleasantly surprised by how well the education fits me. My creativity is very multifunctional, and I don't limit myself in any ways.

I’m a passionate person who’s primarily expressed creativity through modeling in the past, so getting the chance to work behind the process of projects is a new, different and exciting journey that allows me to apply technical knowledge to creative work.

I’ve always known that I wanted to work with something creative and visual. I’m a quick learner who’s always aspiring to be better and I stay challenging myself. My mission is to do what I love; create art, travel and learn. I'm very inspired my humans, culture, music and art.
