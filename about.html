About me

My name is Diana Kirstine Koroma. I’m 24 y/o living in Copenhagen and started studying multimedia design at Campus Køge, Zealand in summer of 2021. I’ve always known that I wanted to work with something creative and visual. I’m a quick learner who’s always aspiring to be better and I stay challenging myself. My creativity has no limits and I love to create projects, designs and art in all forms - it motivates me! Getting the chance to work with the process of these projects is a new and exciting journey that allows me to apply technical knowledge to creative work.
