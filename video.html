Video

Year – 2021 | Project – Video | Client – None | Programs used – After Effects, Premiere Pro, Media Encoder, Audition, Ai, Ps
Project by: Cecilie Schaarup, Johanne Marcher, Shi Cheung, Sarah Larsen, Diana Koroma

Assignment:

In this theme, the task was to make a self-selected “How-To” video that shows a process from A-Z. My group chose to make a video showing how to make a simple roasted veggie dish. We had a vision from the start of animated vegetables that came to life to add comedy and creativity.

Work process:

We worked with the whole process behind the production of videos. We prepared the script with scene descriptions, story boards and recorded voice-overs. Various animations were made for the video, and then we started the actual recording in one of our group members kitchen. We used two different cameras for wide- and longshots, and realized during the recording that we should have used extra lighting for certain scenes. In addition, it was limited how many could edit - we decided one was to edit on the big screen and the rest of us directed.

What I learned:

My role was to ensure we got a script prepared with the accompanying scene description and storyboards. Besides that, I also appeared as the actor. I gained a lot of new knowledge about production in general. Better knowledge of sound, image composition and light, file formats and editing as well as animations and the important concepts. I used a lot of Ai and Ps, and only got a basic knowledge of Ae and Pr.
